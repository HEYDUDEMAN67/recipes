Smoked salmon

A nice hearty recipe from smoked salmon.

What are the ingredients?

  • 4 pieces of salmon with skin on
  • 2 tsp sea salt
    • 2 tsp brown sugar
    • 1 tsp dried lemon myrtle 1 tsp black pepper

    What are the steps?

    1. In a small bowl, mix together the sea salt, brown sugar, dried lemon myrtle, and black pepper.
    2. Rub the mixture evenly over the salmon pieces.
    3. Place the salmon in the refrigerator and let it rest for at least 2 hours, or preferably overnight

    6. Smoke the salmon for about an hour or until it reaches an internal temperature of 57째C (135째F).
    7. Once done, remove the salmon from the smoker and let it cool for a few minutes.
