Tiramisu

tiramisu a great pick me up with its great layering

What are the ingredients?

  • Ladyfingers 7 oz (200 g)
  • Coffee 7.1 oz (200 g) - at room temperature
  • Mascarpone cheese 1.3 cups (300 g)
  • Sugar 0.9 cup (180 g)
  • Egg whites 4.2 oz (120 g) - (about 4)
  • Egg yolks 2.8 oz (80 g) - (approximately 4)
  • Water 2 tbsp (30 g)

    • What are the steps?

    1. Make a zabaglione in a double boiler or a heatproof glass bowl over barely simmering water by whisking the egg yolks, sugar, and vin santo until thick and creamy.
    2. When cooled, combine the thickened egg yolk mixture with the mascarpone.
    3. Fold the cooled melted chocolate into the egg yolk and mascarpone mixture.
    4. Whisk the heavy cream until firm peaks form.
    5. Gently fold the whipped cream into the chocolate mascarpone mixture.
    6. Dip half of the savoiardi in the coffee one at a time and place them in one layer in a pan.
    7. Spread half of the chocolate mascarpone filling over the first layer of savoiardi.
    8. Repeat with a layer of soaked savoiardi followed by chocolate mascarpone cream. Sieve cocoa powder over the tiramisu and finish with shaved chocolate.
